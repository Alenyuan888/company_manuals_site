    Elastic Customer User Guide — English + বাংলা¶

    Part A — English¶

    1. Purpose¶

    Elastic Customer is the customer master-data form for the Elastic business. It is not an order form or a product form.

    It maintains:

    • customer company and sales ownership;
    • contacts and communication details;
    • billing and banking information;
    • logistics and delivery information.

    Create the Elastic Customer record when a new customer first enters the system. Later forms can select this customer and bring forward the approved customer information.

    2. Users and use cases¶

    • Sales: creates customers and maintains company, ownership, contact and delivery information.
    • Management: reviews customer grades and customer master data.
    • Admin: maintains form structure, permissions and linked-form configuration.

    Use this form when:

    1. a new customer enters the system;
    2. company, sales ownership, contact, banking or address information changes;
    3. product, order, delivery, billing or follow-up records need customer information.

    3. Current process¶

    • This form currently has no workflow.
    • This form currently has no automation assistant.
    • The person entering the record must review it before saving.

    4. Required fields¶

    A red * at the upper-left of a field means it is required.

    Required field Employee guidance
    Company Name Enter the customer's full formal company name, not a short name.
    Responsible Sales Select the salesperson responsible for the customer.
    Sales Account Select the correct system member; it affects permission, ownership and later linked forms.
    Country(Region) Select the customer's correct country or region.

    5. Customer Basic Info¶

    Field Employee guidance
    Customer Code Generated automatically by the system; do not edit manually.
    Company Name Formal full company name used on PI, Contract and other official documents.
    Short Name Internal short name for identification.
    Responsible Sales Responsible salesperson.
    Sales Account Required Member field used for ownership, permission and linked forms.
    Customer Type Select from the options currently shown by the system.
    Customer Source Select from the options currently shown by the system.
    Customer Tag Select from the options currently shown by the system.
    Country(Region) Customer's country or region; enter accurately.
    BIN Business Identification Number; it may be used for Bangladesh customers.
    Factory Address Customer's factory address.
    Office Address Customer's office address.
    Grade Management field; ordinary employees should not change it without approval.

    Do not guess the Customer Code numbering rule or invent dropdown options.

    6. Contact Information¶

    Main contact fields:

    • Main Contact Name
    • Position
    • Phone Number
    • E-mail

    Enter one additional person per row in the Other Contacts subform:

    • Name
    • Position
    • Phone
    • Email

    Keep contact names, phone numbers and email addresses as complete and current as possible.

    7. Billing & Banking¶

    Main fields:

    • Bank Name
    • Account No
    • Swift Code
    • Routing
    • Bank Address
    • Bank Country
    • Account Currency
    • Intermediary Bank Required?

    Rules:

    1. Use reliable documents supplied by the customer or bank.
    2. Bank Country is the beneficiary bank's country.
    3. Select Intermediary Bank Required only when the customer or beneficiary bank asks for a correspondent/intermediary bank.
    4. When required, complete one row in Intermediary Bank Details with:
    5. SWIFT/BIC
    6. Bank Name
    7. Bank Address
    8. Country
    9. Currency Served
    10. Never guess unclear banking information. Confirm it with the customer, Finance or Management.

    8. Logistics & Delivery¶

    Main fields:

    • Default Delivery Address
    • Consignee Name
    • Main Contact
    • Position
    • Phone Number
    • E-mail

    Other Contacts subform:

    • Name
    • Position
    • Phone
    • Email

    Keep the default delivery address, consignee and delivery contact accurate. Update the customer master record before using changed information in later delivery documents.

    9. Create a customer¶

    1. Search Company Name, Short Name or Customer Code to avoid a duplicate.
    2. Click New.
    3. Enter the formal full company name in Company Name.
    4. Select the correct Responsible Sales and Sales Account.
    5. Select the correct Country(Region).
    6. Complete type, source, tag, BIN and address fields from verified information.
    7. Enter the main contact and one additional contact per Other Contacts row.
    8. Enter Billing & Banking only when reliable documents are available.
    9. Complete the default delivery address, consignee and logistics contacts.
    10. Review required fields, contacts, banking and delivery information before saving.

    10. Update a customer¶

    1. Find the existing record by Customer Code or Company Name; do not create a duplicate.
    2. Change only information that has been verified.
    3. Confirm the new ownership before changing Responsible Sales or Sales Account.
    4. Use the customer's latest confirmed document when changing bank, phone, email or delivery details.
    5. Management/Admin maintains Grade according to company rules.

    11. Relationships¶

    • Select Elastic Customer in Elastic Update by Designer links to Elastic Customer.
    • The currently confirmed linked fields are Customer Code, Company Name, Responsible Sales and Country(Region).
    • Customer in Elastic Item Master comes from Elastic Customer.
    • Sales Account affects ownership of later customer, product and business data.
    • Exact Elastic Order, Delivery and Billing links remain pending_verification.

    12. Common mistakes and data safety¶

    • Entering a short name in Company Name.
    • Selecting the wrong Responsible Sales, Sales Account or Country(Region).
    • Creating a duplicate customer.
    • Leaving contact or delivery information outdated.
    • Guessing incomplete banking information.
    • Changing Grade without management approval.

    Data safety:

    • Never place real customer names, bank accounts, phone numbers, email addresses or delivery addresses in public manuals, training screenshots or examples.
    • Recheck banking information through the company's internal process before sending it externally.
    • This guide contains field names and operating rules only; it contains no real customer data.

    Part B — বাংলা¶

    ১. Form-এর উদ্দেশ্য¶

    Elastic Customer হলো Elastic business-এর customer master-data form। এটি order form বা product form নয়।

    এই form-এ রাখা হয়:

    • customer company এবং sales ownership;
    • contact ও communication information;
    • billing এবং banking information;
    • logistics এবং delivery information।

    নতুন customer প্রথমবার system-এ এলে আগে Elastic Customer record তৈরি করুন। পরবর্তী form-গুলো এখান থেকে customer নির্বাচন করে অনুমোদিত customer information ব্যবহার করবে।

    ২. কারা এবং কখন ব্যবহার করবেন¶

    • Sales: customer তৈরি করে এবং company, ownership, contact ও delivery information রক্ষণাবেক্ষণ করে।
    • Management: customer Grade এবং master data পরীক্ষা করে।
    • Admin: form structure, permission এবং linked-form configuration রক্ষণাবেক্ষণ করে।

    ব্যবহার করুন যখন:

    1. নতুন customer system-এ আসে;
    2. company, sales ownership, contact, bank বা address information পরিবর্তিত হয়;
    3. product, order, delivery, billing বা follow-up record-এ customer information প্রয়োজন হয়।

    ৩. বর্তমান process¶

    • এই form-এ বর্তমানে workflow নেই।
    • এই form-এ বর্তমানে automation assistant নেই।
    • Save করার আগে data entry করা ব্যক্তি সব information পরীক্ষা করবেন।

    ৪. Required field¶

    Field-এর উপরের-বাম পাশে লাল * থাকলে field-টি required।

    • Company Name: customer-এর formal full company name লিখুন; short name লিখবেন না।
    • Responsible Sales: সঠিক দায়িত্বপ্রাপ্ত salesperson নির্বাচন করুন।
    • Sales Account: সঠিক system member নির্বাচন করুন; এটি permission, ownership এবং পরবর্তী linked form-কে প্রভাবিত করে।
    • Country(Region): customer-এর সঠিক country বা region নির্বাচন করুন।

    ৫. Customer Basic Info¶

    • Customer Code: system automatically generate করে; manually edit করবেন না।
    • Company Name: PI, Contract এবং official document-এ ব্যবহৃত formal full name।
    • Short Name: internal identification-এর জন্য।
    • Responsible Sales: দায়িত্বপ্রাপ্ত salesperson।
    • Sales Account: required Member field; ownership, permission এবং linked form-এর জন্য গুরুত্বপূর্ণ।
    • Customer Type, Customer Source, Customer Tag: system-এ বর্তমানে দেখানো option নির্বাচন করুন।
    • Country(Region): সঠিক country বা region নির্বাচন করুন।
    • BIN: Business Identification Number; Bangladesh customer-এর ক্ষেত্রে ব্যবহার হতে পারে।
    • Factory Address এবং Office Address: verified address লিখুন।
    • Grade: management field; অনুমতি ছাড়া ordinary employee পরিবর্তন করবেন না।

    Customer Code-এর numbering rule বা dropdown option অনুমান করবেন না।

    ৬. Contact Information¶

    Main contact field:

    • Main Contact Name
    • Position
    • Phone Number
    • E-mail

    Other Contacts subform-এ প্রতি row-তে একজন additional contact লিখুন:

    • Name
    • Position
    • Phone
    • Email

    Contact name, phone এবং email যতটা সম্ভব সম্পূর্ণ ও updated রাখুন।

    ৭. Billing & Banking¶

    Main field:

    • Bank Name
    • Account No
    • Swift Code
    • Routing
    • Bank Address
    • Bank Country
    • Account Currency
    • Intermediary Bank Required?

    নিয়ম:

    1. customer বা bank-এর reliable document ব্যবহার করুন।
    2. Bank Country হলো beneficiary bank-এর country।
    3. customer বা beneficiary bank intermediary/correspondent bank চাইলে তবেই Intermediary Bank Required নির্বাচন করুন।
    4. প্রয়োজন হলে Intermediary Bank Details subform-এ SWIFT/BIC, Bank Name, Bank Address, Country এবং Currency Served লিখুন।
    5. অস্পষ্ট banking information অনুমান করবেন না; customer, Finance বা Management-এর সাথে যাচাই করুন।

    ৮. Logistics & Delivery¶

    Main field:

    • Default Delivery Address
    • Consignee Name
    • Main Contact
    • Position
    • Phone Number
    • E-mail

    Other Contacts subform:

    • Name
    • Position
    • Phone
    • Email

    Default delivery address, consignee এবং delivery contact সঠিক রাখুন। Delivery information পরিবর্তিত হলে পরবর্তী delivery document ব্যবহারের আগে customer master record update করুন।

    ৯. নতুন customer তৈরি¶

    1. duplicate এড়াতে Company Name, Short Name বা Customer Code দিয়ে search করুন।
    2. New ক্লিক করুন।
    3. Company Name-এ formal full company name লিখুন।
    4. সঠিক Responsible Sales এবং Sales Account নির্বাচন করুন।
    5. সঠিক Country(Region) নির্বাচন করুন।
    6. verified information থেকে type, source, tag, BIN এবং address পূরণ করুন।
    7. main contact লিখুন এবং Other Contacts-এর প্রতি row-তে একজন contact দিন।
    8. reliable document থাকলে Billing & Banking পূরণ করুন।
    9. default delivery address, consignee এবং logistics contact পূরণ করুন।
    10. Save করার আগে required field, contact, bank এবং delivery information পরীক্ষা করুন।

    ১০. Existing customer update¶

    1. Customer Code বা Company Name দিয়ে existing record খুঁজুন; duplicate তৈরি করবেন না।
    2. শুধু verified information পরিবর্তন করুন।
    3. Responsible Sales বা Sales Account পরিবর্তনের আগে নতুন ownership নিশ্চিত করুন।
    4. bank, phone, email বা delivery information পরিবর্তনের সময় customer-এর latest confirmed document ব্যবহার করুন।
    5. Company rule অনুযায়ী Management/Admin Grade রক্ষণাবেক্ষণ করবে।

    ১১. অন্যান্য form-এর সাথে সম্পর্ক¶

    • Elastic Update by Designer-এর Select Elastic Customer, Elastic Customer-এর সাথে linked।
    • বর্তমানে confirmed linked field: Customer Code, Company Name, Responsible Sales এবং Country(Region)।
    • Elastic Item Master-এর Customer, Elastic Customer থেকে আসে।
    • Sales Account পরবর্তী customer, product এবং business data ownership-কে প্রভাবিত করে।
    • Elastic Order, Delivery এবং Billing-এর exact link pending_verification।

    ১২. সাধারণ ভুল এবং data safety¶

    • Company Name-এ short name লেখা।
    • ভুল Responsible Sales, Sales Account বা Country(Region) নির্বাচন করা।
    • duplicate customer তৈরি করা।
    • পুরোনো contact বা delivery information রেখে দেওয়া।
    • অসম্পূর্ণ banking information অনুমান করা।
    • management approval ছাড়া Grade পরিবর্তন করা।

    কোনো public manual, training screenshot বা example-এ real customer name, bank account, phone, email বা delivery address ব্যবহার করবেন না।

    Admin reference / অ্যাডমিন রেফারেন্স¶

    pending_verification¶

    Do not guess the following items / নিচের বিষয় অনুমান করবেন না:

    • Exact Customer Code numbering formula and rule.
    • Exact Jodoo component types except the confirmed Sales Account = Member field.
    • Complete option lists for Customer Type, Customer Source, Customer Tag, Country(Region), Grade and Account Currency.
    • Exact Publish permission, Actions, Fields, Data Permission and view configuration.
    • Exact Elastic Order, Delivery and Billing reference conditions and field mappings.
    • If an existing or new automation is found later, verify its trigger, actions and mappings before documenting it.
