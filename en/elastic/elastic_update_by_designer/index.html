    Elastic Update by Designer User Guide — English¶

    1. Purpose¶

    Elastic Update by Designer is a workflow form for collecting or supplementing Elastic product information and Common BOM Details for a specified customer and Sales Account.

    After Sales Department confirms the record, the enabled automation adds a new record to Elastic Item Master. This form does not directly edit an existing Item Master record.

    2. Users¶

    • Designer Department: selects customer and Sales Account, then enters product information and Common BOM.
    • Sales Department: reviews all information and selects Confirm or Reject.
    • Jodoo Admin: investigates workflow, permission, formula or automation problems.

    Access and data scope:

    • Sales - Elastic Update Records: used by Marketing Department.
    • Designer - My Elastic Update by Designer: used by Production Department.
    • Designer data scope is controlled by Sales Account = Current User.

    3. Current workflow¶

    1. Designer Editing
    2. Sales Review & Confirmation
    3. End

    Designer should not edit Sales Confirmation, Reject Reason or Approval Time. Sales must provide Reject Reason when rejecting.

    4. Entry principles¶

    • Select Elastic Customer is linked to the Elastic Customer form.
    • After selection, Customer Code, Company Name, Responsible Sales and Country(Region) are filled automatically.
    • Sales Account is a required Member field. Its selectable users come from Marketing Department.
    • A red * at the upper-left of a field means the field is required.
    • For dropdown fields, use the options currently shown by the system.
    • Complete product information as fully as practical.
    • Enter Common BOM Details truthfully. Unknown information may be left blank and must not be guessed.
    • Global BOM string collection, FBS single-row BOM packing and similar fields are system calculation/packing fields. Do not edit them manually.

    5. Review field display rules¶

    • When Sales Confirmation = Confirm, Approval Time is displayed.
    • When Sales Confirmation = Reject, Reject Reason is displayed.
    • Enter Reject Reason when rejecting.

    6. Common BOM Details¶

    The current field names include material selection/identity, No. of Ends, Unit Consumption (g/m), dyeing details, color references, quality references, Light Source, Remarks, Confirm Status and a packed BOM field.

    Common BOM Details synchronizes to Sample Composition Breakdown (OS) and Sample Composition Breakdown (FBS) in Elastic Item Master. Field names remain the same, and fields with the same name are synchronized.

    7. Designer steps¶

    1. Open Elastic Update by Designer and click New.
    2. Select the customer in Select Elastic Customer.
    3. Check Customer Code, Company Name, Responsible Sales and Country(Region).
    4. Select the correct Sales Account.
    5. Enter product type, description, color, width, unit, net weight and remarks.
    6. Enter all known Common BOM Details, one material per row.
    7. Check material, unit, ends, consumption, dyeing and quality references.
    8. Do not edit review fields or system-packed fields.
    9. Submit to Sales Review & Confirmation.

    8. Sales review¶

    1. Review customer and Sales Account.
    2. Review product specification and units.
    3. Review every Common BOM row.
    4. Choose Confirm only when information is correct and complete.
    5. Choose Reject when information is wrong or incomplete, then complete Reject Reason.

    9. Result after review¶

    The automation is enabled. On Update Record, the currently confirmed conditions are Sales Confirmation = Confirm and Sales Account is not empty.

    The assistant adds a new Elastic Item Master record. Common BOM Details synchronizes to Sample Composition Breakdown (OS) and Sample Composition Breakdown (FBS); fields with the same name are synchronized. Reject does not synchronize.

    10. Common mistakes¶

    • Customer is not selected, or Sales Account is empty/incorrect.
    • Width, weight or Unit Consumption uses the wrong unit.
    • Unknown BOM information is guessed.
    • Designer edits review fields.
    • Sales confirms without checking the BOM.
    • Sales rejects without entering Reject Reason.
    • A user expects this form to edit an existing Item Master record.
